I am Zuzana, a Database Manager, Data Analyst and Platform Specialist (Skillato) at Creár (Verona, Italy).

About Me

I am currently secialising in data analysis, database management and administration, platform management, and client relations, with a focus on leveraging technology for user engagement, e-learning optimisation, and business solutions.

Former academic researcher at the (Università degli Studi di Milano Bicocca) with expertise in user behavior analysis and information credibility, especially in the context of AI-generated content. Investigated user behaviour in the context of assessing the information credibility and to develop a user model based on collected interaction data. The model can be used on the downstream application of guiding the detection of false information through automatic credibility judgment. Supervised by Prof. Gabriella Pasi and Prof. Marco Viviani.

Obtained a PhD in Computer & Information Sciences with a specialisation in brain signal processing and data analysis of complex user behaviour in the area of human-information interaction.
